    Treatment & Medication Guide
    💨 Inhalers & Bronchodilators
    Help open airways and reduce inflammation. Short-acting (SABA) for quick relief; long-acting (LABA/LAMA) for daily management.
    🫁 COPD / Spirometry
    Spirometry measures airflow to diagnose obstruction or restriction. COPD is managed with bronchodilators, steroids, and pulmonary rehab.
    🩻 Low-Dose CT (LDCT)
    Recommended for high-risk individuals. LDCT screening detects early lung nodules before symptoms appear — annual screening advised for 50–80-year-old smokers.
    👩‍⚕️ Pulmonologist Referral
    A lung specialist who manages chronic respiratory disease, reads CT/X-rays, and coordinates specialist care including biopsies if needed.
    🧬 Genetic Counselling
    If Alpha-1 Antitrypsin deficiency (AAT) is suspected, a blood test confirms the diagnosis. Augmentation therapy can slow progression.
    🌬️ Pulmonary Rehab
    Supervised exercise and education programme proven to reduce breathlessness, improve quality of life, and cut hospital admissions in COPD patients.
